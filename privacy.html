1. Overview

Speech Pacer is designed to work without collecting or selling personal data. The app does not require an account and does not use advertising SDKs.

2. Data We Handle

On-device only (stored locally)

  • Script text you paste into the app
  • Section list and per-section durations
  • Last selected section index (optional)
  • App settings related to pacing

These items are stored locally on your device (e.g., via SharedPreferences) to improve usability. We do not transmit this content to our servers.

Not collected

  • No name, email, phone number
  • No location data
  • No microphone or audio recording
  • No analytics / tracking identifiers

3. Permissions

  • Vibration: Used to cue section transitions.
  • Screen awake: Used during pacing to prevent screen sleep.

No other permissions are required.

4. Third-Party Services

Speech Pacer does not integrate third-party analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs. App distribution is handled by Apple App Store / Google Play, which may process purchase and device information under their own policies.

5. Data Retention

Local data remains on your device until you delete it by removing the app or clearing app data via your device settings.

6. Children

The app is not directed to children under the minimum age required by applicable law for consent. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to the app. The effective date will be updated accordingly.
